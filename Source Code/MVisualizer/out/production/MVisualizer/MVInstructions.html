Important Keys Notes:

  • Press Esc to exit program (Shift+Ctrl+Q to force close)
  • Press Q to bring this window up again.
  • Press Z to open Exception Logger (Ctrl+C to clear log)

Music Visualizer Controls:

  • Press C to change the visualizer transform (Only on 4th visualizer type)
  • Press M to mute or un-mute the visualizer.
  • Press S to toggle dynamic background squares.
  • Press T to toggle particle friction.
  • Press V to change the song.
  • Press X to change the visualizer type.
  • Press SPACE to pause or play the visualizer
